The FreeStyle Libre flash glucose monitoring system and the FreeStyle LibreLink app (“App”), when used with the FreeStyle Libre sensor, are indicated for measuring interstitial fluid glucose levels in adults aged 18 years and older with diabetes mellitus. If you are using the FreeStyle LibvreLink app, you must also have access to a blood glucose monitoring system as the app does not provide one.
